About Me
  • My research integrates Machine Learning with Computer Graphics and Computer Vision, building end-to-end systems that combine physics-based simulation, geometry, and image/video processing.
  • My work spans 3D reconstruction of the face, body, and hands, along with neural rendering, XR (VR/AR), and haptics.
  • I have peer-reviewed publications in both Computer Graphics and Medical Imaging.
  • I currently work on efficient deep learning-based 4D medical image/video compression, aimed at making high-dimensional data practical to store and analyze.
