Multi-Camera Calibration calibrates multiple cameras using a planar calibration checkerboard through four main steps:
  1. detect checkerboard corners,
  2. remove outlier corners from the previous step (VAE, variational auto-encoder),
  3. estimate initial camera parameters and world points,
  4. refine the initial estimates (bundle adjustment),
  5. optionally, analyze the calibration result.
