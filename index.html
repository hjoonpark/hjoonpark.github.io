Introduction

I am a Ph.D. student in School of Computing at The University of Utah, under the supervision of Prof. Ladislav Kavan. I received my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering (under the supervision of Prof. Dongjun Lee) at Seoul National University, and B.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Korea University, in South Korea.

My research interest is physics-based simulations in computer graphics, combined with machine learning and computer vision.
